RECYCLING PLASTICS INTO JEWELLERY

Plastic bags are a major environmental concern as they contribute to pollution and take a long time to decompose.
Recycling them into jewelry helps reduce the overall amount of plastic waste in landfills and oceans.

By repurposing plastic bags into fashionable accessories, we minimize the need for new raw materials, such as metals or gemstones,
thereby conserving natural resources.
