Going Beyond Basics

As you delve deeper into HTML, consider exploring the following topics:

  • Links and Navigation:

    Create hyperlinks with the <a> tag. Use attributes like href to link to other pages or external resources. Understand the concept of relative and absolute paths.

  • Images and Multimedia:

    Enhance your content by incorporating images with the <img> tag. Explore multimedia elements like audio and video to make your webpage more engaging.

  • List Structures:

    Master the art of using lists to organize information. Utilize ordered lists (<ol>) for numbered sequences and unordered lists (<ul>) for bulleted items.

  • Forms:

    Create interactive forms with the <form> tag. Explore form elements such as text inputs, checkboxes, radio buttons, and submit buttons. Use attributes like action and method.

Remember, the beauty of HTML lies in its simplicity and power to structure content. Happy coding!
