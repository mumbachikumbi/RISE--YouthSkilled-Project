ABOUT

Welcome to Youth Skilled, an innovative platform dedicated to addressing Zambia's unemployment crisis. Our platform aims to empower the youth by providing essential skills through carefully designed courses. Join us as we equip the younger generation with the necessary tools to thrive in the ever-changing job landscape and contribute to solving Zambia's unemployment challenges.

BUILDING A COMMUNITY

Youth Skilled is more than a platform – it's a thriving community where like-minded individuals come together to collaborate and grow.

OUR MISSION

Empowering Zambia's youth through skillful education, Youth Skilled is on a mission to defeat unemployment by providing essential skills. Join us in fostering a future where every young individual is equipped to succeed, contributing to a brighter, more empowered Zambia.
